About Me

I’m Engineer Rhine — a Computer Engineer and Certified Safety Officer II with a strong focus on building secure, efficient systems that blend advanced technology with real-world safety standards. I believe that great engineering doesn't just solve problems — it protects people, optimizes performance, and drives progress.

I earned my degree from Pangasinan State University – Urdaneta City Campus, majoring in System Network Administration under the Computer Engineering program. My background spans a broad technical range, including Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing, Network Engineering, Data Analytics, and Cybersecurity. I'm also trained in mastering tools like ChatGPT for intelligent system design, automation, and innovation.

Alongside my technical training, I’ve developed a strong understanding of business and marketing strategies. This helps me approach projects not just as an engineer, but as a problem-solver who sees the bigger picture — from planning to execution to real-world application.

I’m also proud to take on leadership roles that allow me to contribute to the growth of my peers and community. I formerly serve as the Public Relations Officer (PRO) for both the Computer Engineering Graduating Officers and the Federated Graduating Class. I'm also the Business Manager of the College of Engineering and Architecture Student Council at PSU Urdaneta Campus. In previous years, I served as the Assistant Secretary of ICpEP.SE, 3rd Year Representative for the Peer Facilitator Circle, and a standing Computer Engineering Representative in the Supreme Student Council.
